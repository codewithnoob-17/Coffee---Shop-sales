Project Objective

The objective of this project was to analyze coffee shop sales data and generate actionable business insights related to sales performance, customer behavior, store performance, product demand, and peak business hours.

Dashboard KPIs

Total Footfall: 149,116

Business Questions & Answers
1. How do sales vary by day of the week and hour of the day?
By Hour
Sales activity starts increasing from 7 AM.
Peak customer activity is observed between 8 AM and 10 AM.
The highest order volume occurs around 10 AM.
After 11 AM, sales gradually decline throughout the day.
Very low sales are recorded after 7 PM.
By Weekday
Friday (21,701) recorded the highest footfall.
Thursday (21,654) and Monday (21,643) also showed strong customer traffic.
Saturday (20,510) recorded the lowest footfall.
2. Are there any peak times for sales activity?

Yes.

Peak Hours
8 AM – 10 AM is the busiest sales period.
Maximum orders are generated around 10 AM.
Peak Days
Friday is the highest-performing day.
Thursday and Monday are also strong sales days.

Key Business Recommendations
Increase staffing during 8 AM – 10 AM peak hours.
Focus promotional campaigns on Coffee and Tea categories.
Maintain adequate inventory for Barista Espresso and Chai Tea.
Introduce upselling strategies to increase the Average Bill Per Person.
Replicate successful sales strategies from Hell's Kitchen across other stores.
